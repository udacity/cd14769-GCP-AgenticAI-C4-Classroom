## Shipping Policy

1. Standard Shipping:
   - Cost: $5.00
   - Delivery Time: 5-7 business days.
   - Free for orders over $50.

2. Express Shipping:
   - Cost: $10.00
   - Delivery Time: 2-3 business days.

3. International Shipping:
   - Cost: $20.00
   - Delivery Time: 10-15 business days.

## Returns Policy

1. You must return items within the time period allowed for your state.
   - The return period is based on the state where we ship the item.
   - See the table below to determine the return period
2. Items must be in original condition.
3. Return shipping is free for defective items. For other reasons, a $5 restocking fee applies.

Return Period:
* NY and CA: 90 days
* TX: 45 days
* All other US states: 30 days
* International orders: 60 days


## Tracking

1. Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email.
2. You can track your order on our website using the order ID.
