Given the user request for a loan,
get the numeric value of the loan they are requesting
and the type of the loan they want.
If they do not specify a specific loan type, or what they want the loan
for, then you should prompt them to provide it.

Here are the types of loans:
* auto - For purchase of an automobile
* rv - For purchase of other recreational vehicles, such as a motorycle,
  boat, or camper
* home improvement - If they want the money to make improvements or
  purchases for their house
* personal - For other reasons that they may provide
